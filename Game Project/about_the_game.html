"Bob's Journey" is a rougelike video game. In roguelike games you play as a certain character which has to make his way through some type of a dungeon. These game usually contain random generated levels, turn- based gameplay, tile- based graphics and permament death of your character.

The game is centered around you, Bob. Bob is a magicican, he lives in a village full of magicians. Bob works as a blacksmith who makes swords. One day the village leader, the most powerful of all the magicians, wants to have a sword and Bob accepts the offer but the sword has to be special, it has to have magic.

Because of that wish, Bob sets on a journey to find the best type of magic to use on the sword but. He finally founds a place which could contain that magic but the problem is that this place is a dungeon full of frightening creatures but Bob is fearless so he enters the dungeon bravely and this is when his real journey begins.
